## Context

What is the situation? What forces are in play (technical, organizational,
political, time)? What other paths did we genuinely consider?

Keep this section dense. The point is *why now* and *why this is hard*,
not a tutorial on the problem domain.

## Decision

What did we decide to do? One short paragraph. If it's longer than 3
sentences, you're probably hedging.

Optional: bullet list of the concrete artifacts this decision creates
(e.g. "this introduces a new `plugins/<kind>/` directory").

## Consequences

What becomes easier? Harder? What are we locked into? What new failure
modes does this create? What did we explicitly *not* solve?

Include the "yes but" answers to the obvious challenges. A reader should
finish this section knowing both why we did it and why someone might
reasonably disagree.
